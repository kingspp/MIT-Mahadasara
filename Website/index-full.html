How to get to the venue

Route from Railway Station

Nearest station: Mysore Railway Station

The College is 10km from city bus stand. Train facilities are provided by Indian Railways. Shuttle trains are being provided between Bangalore, Mandya to Mysore
Directions

Route from bus stand

Nearest stand: Nagunhalli Bus stand

The College is 9km from city bus stand. The bus facilities are provided by KSRTC. Shuttle buses are provided across Nagunhalli and Belwadi stations.
Directions

Route from hotel

Nearest Hotel: The Atrium

Revel in the lap of luxury and discover the spirit of Mysore only at The Atrium Boutique Hotel which stands as a calm tranquil oasis on the Bangalore Mysore State Highway.
