As a part of MAHADASARA 2015, an annual college fest of Maharaja Institute of Technology, a MARATHON has been organized on February 8th 2015, commemorating the 8th Year anniversary of the institution. The theme for the MARATHON is “SWACHH BHARATH”. The span of the run is 8km, which starts from C.P.C Polytechnic and ends at M.I.T Mysore campus. The winner of the fest will be awarded a cash prize of Rs.8000/-. The SWACHH BHARATH campaign will be conducted in the surrounding villages of M.I.T Mysore campus after the MARATHON ends.
