In order to participate in the MARATHON-
1. Register online in the website www.mahadasara.mitmysore.in
2. After registering,please note down the Registration ID number for further processing.
3. Go to any one of the registration desk or call the volunteers & pay the appropriate fee.
4. Collect the Receipt from the volunteers & also a Registration Card from them.
5. Congratulations! You are a registered participant for the MARATHON.










Distance: 8 KM
