General Instructions

Techno Management

  • Each department is eligible to represent two teams (7 members in a team) and one faculty member.
  • Participants of one event cannot participate in another event*
  • Vulgarity or imitation leads to disqualification of the team.
  • For all events Judges Decision will be final.
  • Participants should be present at the venue 10 minutes prior to the schedule.
  • Dress Code – Formals (Changes is allowed based on the events*)
  • Needs required for events: Laptops, Gadgets, Stationary, Properties etc. must be carried by the participants.
  • Spot registration will not be entertained.
